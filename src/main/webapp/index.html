World Charities

The purpose of this web application is to make a platform for all charities around the world to introduce themselves to the public. Many charities have websites, but most people still do not know about it, or they do not where to find information about different charities.

Our goal is to help people find information about many different charities on one website. This way, we hope to help charities save advertising money for helping more people in need.

Charities can post events, volunteer recruting, and other information here for everyone to view and connect with them.
